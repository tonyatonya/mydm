Behind MYDM

Phai Subhawita Klunson, a graphic designer, a homemaker, a mother of one cute son, has worked in graphic design for several years, creating remarkable design for her clients many industries. But design is not only found in her job, it can be found in every piece of her house, every meal that she cooks for family, and every cloth that she wears, and all are with her heart. So keen on small details, she is in love with hand making everything from note books to vases. Now her love of graphic design and handmade is combined to create a perfectly design pieces for your house and lifestyle.

So MYDM is created from the mix of her passion on design and handcraft, and also, of course, pattern. It is found to inspire people’s life by creatively lively pattern for everyday use.

“ There is something amazing about pattern, it’s imperfectly perfect. When u move a piece just slightly, everything changes.
When you darken the shade just a bit, the mood changes. It’s fun to make it just right, and when you got a perfect pattern, it’s never perfect. Pattern and colour can light up our mood, and get us inspired. FO”
