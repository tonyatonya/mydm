ABOUT
MYDM : Design Matters.
MYDM, by Phai Subhawita Klunson, is a Bangkok-based graphic design house offering a variety of creative branding and design services for a wide range of private and corporate clients who value our refined and detail visuals. To get the finest execution,we craft the concept and develop beautiful and honest graphic out of our sincere care. We are also a home to Crafting and Design Community; we'd love to share our belief to others. MYDM items are designed to stimulate ones' creative thinking and empower them to make their mark of how to create the happy home living lifestyle in the crazy world.

WHAT WE DO

We are a boutique branding agency whose clients include companies from various industries around the country and internationally. We create the look and feel of businesses, organizations and product lines. We enjoy collaboration and long-term relationships with our clients, creating everything from logotypes to identities to alluring websites—paying close attention to the small details along the way. The end result is a well crafted brand a powerful tool for a company's success.

WORK PROCESS
Learn a little more about
OUR TAILOR-MADE APPROACH

​STEP 1 : We like to have a conversation (face-to-face, if you're nearby) to ﬁnd out all about your business—where you've been and where you're going. You meet our team of designers and project manager, and we do further research to gain understanding and ﬁnd inspiration. In this ﬁrst step, we conﬁrm everyone's expectations and the direction of the work.

​​​STEP 2 : The hands-on design phase is next. MYDM brings its team of designers together to develop overall strategies and specific design concepts for your review. Client feedback is an essential part of creating great brands, and we invite input throughout the process. We're pleased to advise on the big ideas and the details and invite you to share your inspiration with us.

STEP 3 : With design decisions made, production and implementation begins. As needed for each project, MYDM provides full-service coordination — planning and direct oversight for printing, website programming, brand execution and more. We are as excited as our clients to see the tangible and virtual results as we complete each piece and see your brand unfold.
